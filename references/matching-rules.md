# Matching and restructuring rules

## Semantic roles

Classify content as `cover`, `executive_summary`, `chapter_divider`, `explanation`, `framework`, `comparison`, `data_overview`, `single_chart`, `single_kpi`, `case_study`, `quote`, `risk`, `checklist`, or `closing`.

## Mandatory split

Split when any condition holds: ordinary title cannot fit two lines after concise rewriting; two independent conclusions; over 180 English words or 300 Chinese characters; over five KPIs; bar chart over eight categories; table over seven columns or over three main items per column; long prose plus complex chart plus long quote; over three reading paths; or footnotes invade content.

## Permitted merge

Merge adjacent short points supporting one conclusion; a title plus one/two sentences when not a divider/breathing page; adjacent objective/action and results into T19; or two small charts sharing one conclusion into one primary chart plus numeric annotation.

## Compression

Preserve facts, numbers, units, dates, qualifiers, and sources. Consolidate duplicates. Group 8-12 peer bullets into 3-5 groups. Do not change data definitions or invent missing business facts.
