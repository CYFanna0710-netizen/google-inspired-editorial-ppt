# T01-T23 layout library

All templates use the 12-column grid and one primary claim. Fields not supplied remain empty; never fabricate filler.

| ID | Purpose | Required fields | Layout and density budget | Constraints |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T01 | Cover | title, optional subtitle/year | oversized left title, edge mesh or E01 integrated emotional windows; low | title 10-22 CJK chars or 6-14 words; subtitle <=2 lines |
| T02 | Executive summary | title, 2-3 blocks, KPI | two text columns + right KPI; medium | 300-450 CJK chars or 180-260 words |
| T03 | Key insights | title, 5-7 metrics | full dark, top color band, metric row; high | one number + one sentence each |
| T04 | Background | title, short body | upper-left text, lower-right mesh, or E03 editorial story split; low | breathing page |
| T05 | Methodology | title, scope blocks | left explanation, right scope/map area; medium | one focal point per group |
| T06 | Contents | title, 3-5 chapters | large left label, right list; low | use two columns above five |
| T07 | Chapter divider | chapter, title, optional subtitle | continuous gradient system or E02 central emotional portal; low | no body/chart/list; adjacent solid color panels forbidden |
| T08 | Claim + KPI + definition | title, body, KPI, definition | 7/5 with KPI and definition; medium | title <=4 lines, body <=180 CJK chars |
| T09 | Three-stage framework | title, 3 columns | three editable panels; high | exactly three peer stages |
| T10 | Full quote | quote, attribution | large quote with edge mesh or restrained E06/E07 image rhythm; low | 35-90 CJK chars or 20-55 words |
| T11 | Explanation + quote | title, body, quote | 5/7 or 6/6 split; medium | optional single KPI |
| T12 | Three-way comparison | title, 3 groups | three panels with colored headers; high | 4-7 short rows each |
| T13 | Text + dark chart | title, body, chart | white 7/5 + dark chart; high | one main chart |
| T14 | Data matrix | title, 5-7 columns | gradient header + gray cells; high | max 7 x 3 |
| T15 | Conclusion + checklist | title, conclusion, 3-5 items | left conclusion, right list; medium | each item <=2 lines |
| T16 | Domain index | title, 3-6 items | left context, right numbered list; medium | 3-6 items |
| T17 | KPI overview | title, 3-5 metrics | equal metric columns; high | uniform number format |
| T18 | Topic data | title, body, <=2 KPIs, chart | white left + dark right; high | chart <=6 bars |
| T19 | Case study | objective, action, 2-3 outcomes, optional quote | 2x2 editorial zones or E03 asymmetric evidence cluster; medium | preserve attribution |
| T20 | Super KPI | metric, explanation, case | huge number + case panel; low | one primary KPI |
| T21 | Risk | title, evidence, optional quote | explanation + restrained warning block; medium | no alarm-red treatment |
| T22 | Two-column checklist | title, 6-8 items | two gray columns; medium | 3-4 items per column |
| T23 | Closing CTA | title, CTA/contact | full dark + blue-violet diffuse bloom + edge mesh, or E06 emotional reprise; low | no research detail; no hard multicolor stripe as primary visual |
